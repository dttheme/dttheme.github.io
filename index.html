Projects

Rumblr

Rumblr allows users to input an amount of time and a minimum magnitude. It then returns the number of earthquakes in the specified time and magnitude range. This information is populated on the 3D-rendered globe, with markers at the specific coordinates and popups with additional details. News stories from the time period specified are featured on the right side.

Tech: JQuery, HTML, CSS & WebGL Earth API

MoodRing

MoodRing is an application that is part survey, part journal and part mirror. It allows the user to reflect on their day and then later look back at that information to gain a better understanding of how they are feeling from day to day, as well as which activities or thoughts associate with which moods.

Tech: JQuery, HTML, CSS, Node/Express, MongoDB, JWT, Mocha & Chai
